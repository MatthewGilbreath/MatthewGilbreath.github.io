Poker Table

A handcrafted poker table built for weekly games with friends. The oval tabletop is wrapped in casino‑grade felt and features integrated cup holders and a padded rail. Under‑mounted LED lights provide subtle ambience during play.

  • Solid hardwood frame with removable leg assembly
  • Casino‑grade felt surface for smooth card slides
  • Padded rail upholstered in faux leather with built‑in cup holders
  • LED lighting strip controlled by remote for ambience

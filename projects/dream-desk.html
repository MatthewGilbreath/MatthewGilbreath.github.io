Dream Desk

This project was born out of necessity: I needed a workspace that fit my unique workflow. I designed and built a custom L‑shaped desk from hardwood and steel, incorporating integrated tool storage, a pegboard system, and cable management throughout. The result is a functional, minimalistic space that inspires productivity.

  • Custom L‑shaped hardwood desktop with steel legs
  • Integrated tool storage drawers and compartments
  • Pegboard wall for organisation and easy access to tools
  • Built‑in cable management to keep the workspace tidy

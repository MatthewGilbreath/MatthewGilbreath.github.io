Recipe Database

As a line cook and food enthusiast, I found myself juggling countless recipes. To streamline prep work, inventory and menu planning, I developed a digital recipe database using Notion. It tracks ingredients, costs, and cooking times, all in one place.

  • Built in Notion for accessibility across devices
  • Organised recipes by cuisine, ingredients and difficulty
  • Integrated cost and yield tracking for budgeting
  • Shared with team members to standardise prep across shifts

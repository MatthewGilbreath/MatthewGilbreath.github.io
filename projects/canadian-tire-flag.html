Canadian Tire Flag

For a community event hosted by a local Canadian Tire, I designed and constructed a large Canadian flag display. The project required precise patterning and sewing of heavy outdoor fabric, as well as a collapsible aluminium frame for transport.

  • Hand‑cut and sewed red and white panels from weatherproof fabric
  • Fabricated an aluminium frame that collapses for easy transport
  • Added grommets and tie‑downs to secure the flag in windy conditions
  • Coordinated installation for the community event

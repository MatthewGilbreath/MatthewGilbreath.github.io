Squirrel Feeder

This playful project brings wildlife closer while keeping squirrels off the bird feeder. The feeder’s spinning arm discourages unwanted pests, and a sloped roof sheds rain and snow. Constructed from cedar, it weathers beautifully outdoors.

  • Cedar construction for weather resistance and longevity
  • Spinning arm to prevent pests from accessing food
  • Sloped roof to keep feed dry in rain or snow
  • Easy‑fill hopper with hinged lid
